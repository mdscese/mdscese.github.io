About Me

Hello! I'm Marcus Scese, a recent graduate from Michigan Technological University, where I earned my Masters in Computer Science. My passion lies in the realms of vision and graphics systems, although I have a keen interest in machine learning as well.

Throughout my academic journey, I've delved into research areas such as API systems, big data control, and quantum computation, aiming to contribute to the advancement of technology in these fields.

Beyond the confines of academia, I find joy in various activities that fuel my creativity and curiosity. Whether it's immersing myself in the virtual worlds of video games with friends, tinkering with and customizing small electronics like game controllers, or getting my hands dirty while working on my car, I'm always eager to explore new challenges.

Above all of those hobbies though when winter arrives and I have free time, you'll usually find me strapping into a snowboard. Snowboarding has become my favorite pastime since a friend introduced me to it at Michigan Tech.

As for how I am right now, I am looking forward to the adventures ahead!

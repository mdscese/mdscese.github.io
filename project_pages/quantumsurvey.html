Quantum Computation Survey
❯

Description:

Submitted in the fall of 2023, this paper delivers a thorough examination of quantum simulation methodologies and quantum simulators tailored for classical computing systems. Our study commences with an extensive analysis encompassing over 150 simulators and quantum libraries. From this vast pool, we meticulously curate a selection, spotlighting simulators that are not only actively maintained but also proficient in simulating quantum algorithms involving more than 10 qubits. Through our investigation, a striking trend emerges: the most efficient and actively supported simulators predominantly originate from developments post-2010. Additionally, we present a comprehensive taxonomy delineating the fundamental simulation methods pivotal to this burgeoning field.

https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.16505
